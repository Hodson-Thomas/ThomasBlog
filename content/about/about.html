  • MJCraft Minecraft server
    From January 2019 to July 2022

    MJCcraft is a Minecraft multi-player server. This project first started in 2019 with the MJC-CS (House of Youth and Culture - Social Centre) of Chenôve. The purpose was to promote the creation of video club like ours to encourage players to socialise instead of isolating themselves. Our group recently split up (due to higher education studies), so me and another member of the group (his github), decided to continue this project during our free time. We programmed plugins for the game to extend it. I mainly work on the front-end of the website and on the in-game facilities for the updates (building structures, etc…). You can find a couple videos of this server in this YouTube playlist. Today the server is closed.

  • Cansat
    From September 2020 to October 2021

    This is a competition, for high school students, organised by ESA (European Space Agency). The purpose is to bring to life your own space agency by inventing and creating a satellite inside a soda can. This satellite must accomplish two missions:

    • The first one is imposed by the organisers.
    • The teams choose their secondary mission.

    I mainly worked as a software engineer. I was also the report writer of the team. After one year of work, we were titled as the French champions, and we had the honour to represent our country in the European phase of the competition.

    Project members :

    • Aristide Urli
    • Jiří Gebauer
    • Titouan Drouynot
